Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Repcord handles your information.

Information You Create

Repcord stores the reptile records you create in the app, including feeding logs, weight logs, care logs, health notes, custom labels, and app preferences.

iCloud Sync

If iCloud sync is enabled on your device, Repcord may sync your records through your personal iCloud account so the same data can be available on your Apple devices signed in to the same Apple ID.

Accounts

Repcord does not require you to create a separate account.

Backups

If you export a backup file, that file is created at your request and stored in the location you choose through the iOS share or file system interface.

Purchases

In-app purchases are handled by Apple through StoreKit. Repcord does not receive your payment card details.

Contact
